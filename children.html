Cyber Safety Tips for Kids

Always use strong passwords that no one can guess. If you get a strange message, tell a parent or teacher.

Don't click on links from strangers, and never share your address or school name online.

Never turn on your webcam for strangers. Always ask an adult before joining video calls.

Ask permission before downloading apps or games. Some might not be safe for kids.

If something makes you feel uncomfortable online, always talk to an adult you trust.

Do not believe everything you see online, some things may be fake or dangerous.
